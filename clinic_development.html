Clinical Development

The goal of the Verséa Rx botanical clinical program is to gain IND approval with multiple product candidates. Following IND approval each botanical candidate will undergo pre-clinical toxicologic assessments followed by human trials where Verséa will seeking to be advance one of the first hemp-derived botanical extract-based drug product candidates at the FDA.

Research & Development

Verséa is studying components of the hemp plant individually and in combinations to assess in-vitro and in-vivo response across multiple body systems. Physiologic response to determines therapeutic target triggering cross breeding and chemovar development in the breeding program to match the lab created combinations. This process allows Verséa to build a pipeline of New Chemical Entities and Botanical Rx drug candidates targeting:

  • Pain
  • Inflammation
  • Anxiety and Mood Disorders
  • Disorders of the Central Nervous System
  • Cancer
  • Infectious Disease

Breeding Program

The Verséa breeding program is designed to translate lab created new chemical entities to botanical chemovars that are replicated and scaled for cultivation. Innovative techniques and process will allow for the rapid scale up to produce drug substance and product for clinical trials through the Rx botanical program.
