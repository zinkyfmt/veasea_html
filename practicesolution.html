Practice Solutions

Verséa Practice Solutions bringing patient and practice enhancement services and products to the US healthcare market. Through partnerships, technology and service licenses, Verséa Practice Solutions offerings focus on patient outcomes enhancement, outcomes tracking, education and in-office dispensing services.

Diagnostics

Verséa has strategic partnerships providing access to Bulk Drug Tests, COVID-19 Tests and Molecular Tests to hospitals, private practices and Ambulatory Surgery Centers across the United States. The Verséa Task Force team of experts continues to research and identify the highest quality tests that are made to the highest standards. All the testing offered is FDA Approved or EUN/EUA. Verséa provides personalized customer service. You deal with one Company (Verséa) and have direct access to the latest testing.

Versea Patient Registry

Hospitals, practices and clinics are partnering with Verséa on the recruitment of patients into the nationwide Outcomes Research Registry. The registry is an IRB approved and HIPAA compliant collecting data to provide much needed answers around how patients are using Verséa products. Aggregate data will be made available for analysis, medical publication and release into the public domain.
